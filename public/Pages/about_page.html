About

Welcome to Animo Taro, the online hub for game developers from DLSU! This is a space where students can connect, collaborate, and share their passion for game development. Whether you’re looking to showcase your projects, seek advice, or find teammates for your next game jam, our community is here to support and inspire you. Join us to learn, create, and grow together as the next generation of game developers.
